### Claim 2: competitive_advantage

- **Claim**: Agrium's integrated Wholesale (production) and Retail (distribution) strategy creates counter-cyclical resilience that pure-play distributors and pure-play producers cannot replicate: when nutrient prices fall sharply (-25%+ in 2013), the Retail business captures margin expansion while Wholesale EBITDA declines, generating more stable aggregate earnings through the cycle.
- **Evidence**: Q4 2013 call: Wholesale EBITDA declined significantly due to 'nutrient prices down 25% or more across all three nutrients.' Yet Retail EBITDA reached a record $961M for full-year 2013 with 'comparable growth of 1.7%.' CEO: 'The integrated approach provides earnings stability that a pure wholesaler or pure retailer cannot offer.' Q4 2014 showed continued integration benefit: repatriated 150,000 tonnes of nitrogen and phosphate from US to Western Canada through own retail distribution, optimizing logistics.
